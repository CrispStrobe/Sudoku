CrispSudoku is a polished, ad-free Sudoku game with real depth — four rule variants, six grid sizes, and a built-in tutor that explains how to solve every board.

PLAY YOUR WAY
• Four variants: Classic, Jigsaw (irregular regions), Sudoku-X (diagonals) and Killer (summed cages)
• Six grid sizes: 4×4, 6×6, 8×8, 9×9, 10×10 and 12×12
• Four difficulties, from Easy to Expert
• A new Daily Challenge every day — the same board for everyone

LEARN AS YOU PLAY
• "Explain the solve" walks through any board step by step, naming each technique (naked singles, hidden singles, locked candidates, X-wing and more)
• "Next logical step" hints tell you what to play next, and why
• Every puzzle gets an honest difficulty rating based on the hardest technique it actually requires

THOUGHTFUL TOUCHES
• Pencil-mark notes, unlimited undo, and live conflict highlighting
• A mistake limit that scales with difficulty
• Per-difficulty hint budgets, so hints stay meaningful
• Beautiful themes you unlock as you play
• Achievements, streaks and detailed statistics

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• 100% offline — no account, no ads, no tracking, no data collection
• Every puzzle is guaranteed to have exactly one solution

CrispSudoku is free software, open source under the GNU AGPL-3.0.
