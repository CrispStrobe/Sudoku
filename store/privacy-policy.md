# CrispSudoku — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 30 May 2026_

**CrispSudoku does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** The app
runs entirely on your device and works fully offline.

A hosted copy is served at
**https://sudoku-lac-five.vercel.app/privacy** — use that URL in App Store
Connect and the Google Play Console.

## Data we collect
None. CrispSudoku has no user accounts, no advertising, no analytics, and no
third-party tracking SDKs. Nothing you do in the app is sent to us or any server.

## Data stored on your device
To make the game work, CrispSudoku saves locally on your device only (via the
OS's standard app storage): game progress, statistics (puzzles solved, streaks,
best times), unlocked themes and achievements, daily-puzzle completion, and your
settings. This data never leaves your device, is not linked to your identity,
and is removed when you uninstall the app.

## Permissions and network use
CrispSudoku does not request access to contacts, photos, location, camera, or
microphone, and makes no network requests on its own. Tapping the contact email
or a license link on the About screen opens your mail app or browser — an action
you initiate.

## Children's privacy
Suitable for all ages; collects no data from anyone, including children.

## In-app purchases
If offered in a future version, IAPs are processed by Apple or Google under their
own terms; CrispSudoku does not receive or store payment information.
